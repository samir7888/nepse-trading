## 🛡️ Risk Management Rules (CRITICAL!)

### Position Sizing Rules:

- **Maximum per stock**: 30% of total capital (NPR 900)
- **Recommended per stock**: 20-25% of capital (NPR 600-750)
- **Minimum investment**: NPR 500 (to make commissions worthwhile)

### Stop Loss Rules:

- **Always set stop loss** at 30% below entry price
- **Never ignore stop loss** - this is your safety net
- **Use trailing stops** after 5 weeks if profitable

### Profit Taking Rules:

- **Take profits** at 60% gain (don't get greedy!)
- **Partial profit taking**: Sell 50% at 40% gain, rest at 60%
- **Never hold hoping for more** - stick to the plan

## 🚨 Common Beginner Mistakes to Avoid

### Emotional Mistakes:

❌ **Panic selling** during market dips
❌ **FOMO buying** after big moves
❌ **Holding losers** hoping they'll recover
❌ **Selling winners** too early from fear

### Technical Mistakes:

❌ **Ignoring stop losses** (biggest mistake!)
❌ **Risking too much** per trade
❌ **Not diversifying** (all money in one stock)
❌ **Trading too frequently** (overtrading)

### Money Management Mistakes:

❌ **Investing borrowed money**
❌ **Using emergency fund** for trading
❌ **Not keeping records** of trades
❌ **Increasing position size** after losses
